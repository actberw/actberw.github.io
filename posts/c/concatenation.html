c字符串续行

Thu 21 August 2014

示例: 

char *my_string = "Line 1 \
                   Line 2";

char *my_string = "Line 1 "
                  "Line 2";

注: With a #define, you have to add an extra '\' to concatenate the two strings:

#define kMyString "Line 1"\
                  "Line 2";
